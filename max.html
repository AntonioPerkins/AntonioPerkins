    Mission

    At maxxpower we believe in our customer and providing them with the best possible service we can. We also believe in protecting the community and doing Energy as a 0 waste business.

    About Us

    Founded in 1430 we have been in the business of customer satisfaction for 588 years. Being the largest power company in the country we have an even bigger reputation to uphold. With our services covering the entire 50 states we currently employ over 100,000 people from coast to coast. Maxx Power is a family company, started in the greater Richmond area, we have stayed true to our founding ideals of providing every household with the best possible energy experience.

    Fire

    Since The Fire in 2016 Maxx Power has made an epic comeback. Our company has been able to grow insurmountably since the fire. Like a phoenix from the ashes we have come back afloat and even profited since. We lost a lot from the fire, things that simply can’t be replaced so if you are finally coming back around to needing power again please contact our 24/7 support hotline to start up your monthly power bill again.
