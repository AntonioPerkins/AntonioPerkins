    Outage FAQ's

    (Outage Frequently asked questions)

    1.Why does my neighbor have power and I don’t after a storm?

    Answer: Your neighbor could have a seperate main powerline going into their home. Your main powerline could be down while your neighbor’s is unaffected.

    2.Will there ever be downtime when performing work on my lines?

    Answer: If we are ever performing work that requires the line into your house to be shut off until work is complete we will notify you the day prior to so you can make arrangements to work around it.
